Chairman's Message

Ahmed Bin Khalaf is a successful self-made business leader, who started his career in the oil and gas industry.

His business interest includes ventures relating to leading home grown UAE products and multiple franchises that are popular in the local and regional markets.

Ahmed Bin Khalaf assumed office as the Chairman of Siraj Finance since its acquisition in 2016 by the Trizac Holding LLC, a group entity under the Al Otaiba Group of Companies.

Ahmed Bin Khalaf is a passionate philanthropist who extends his time, effort and resources to the wellbeing of the local community. He has been, and continues to be, instrumental in the development of various social welfare projects, green community initiatives, Emiratisation platforms for the young UAE Nationals and welfare projects for children and senior citizens.

Mr. Ahmed Bin Khalaf Al Otaiba Chairman
